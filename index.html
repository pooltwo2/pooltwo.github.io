What I'm working on

Project: AC

With more than 5 years working in the shadows, project: AC emerges as an independent animated project that has evolved thru sketches and drafts. With a script that is already nearing 90% completion and a creative process that is steadily advancing with 70% completed, the project continues to take shape in every layer of its development, with a story deeply woven by yours truly and my team. Strongly inspired by reinterpreted biblical passages and fused with the darkest essence of Lovecraftian horrors, in this series there are no heroes. There is no redemption. Only fragments of humanity rotting in a city that has already forgotten its name. A condemned city, where faith is sold to the highest bidder and sanity is a luxury reserved for the dead. Here, we do not fight for justice, nor for glory; we fight to not disappear, to not be devoured by that which breathes among the rubble. Welcome to a place where God left a long time ago, and angels no longer descend... or if they do, Assassin's City is not a story of salvation, it is a warning disguised as prophesy, a twisted vision of the end times, a broken mirror where humanity observes its darkest reflection and decides to lie to itself once more.
